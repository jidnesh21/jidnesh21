## 🚀 Featured Projects

### 📊 Retail Sales & Profit Intelligence Dashboard — Power BI | 2026

Designed and developed a two-page Power BI dashboard analyzing $2.3M in retail sales and $286K in profit to identify drivers of margin compression and product-level profitability. Built a star-schema data model with a custom date table and authored DAX measures — including YoY growth, profit margin %, and dynamic Top N/Bottom N ranking — to enable time-intelligence analysis. Designed interactive visualizations (KPI cards, geographic maps, combo charts, conditionally formatted tables) and implemented drillthrough navigation with synchronized slicers for cross-page filtering and self-service exploration, uncovering that the bottom 10 products accounted for losses equal to 44% of top 10 product profits.
